    Port Moresby Nature Park is one of Papua New Guinea’s foremost nature attractions, combining a botanical garden and wildlife sanctuary in one place. It’s located near the University of Papua New Guinea and is celebrated as the only combined botanical and zoological park in the country, showcasing the rich biodiversity of PNG’s tropical environment.
