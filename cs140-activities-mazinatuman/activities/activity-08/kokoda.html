    The Kokoda Trail is a famous trekking route known for its strong mix of adventure, history, and natural beauty.
    The trail is about 96 kilometers long and crosses the rugged Owen Stanley Range, leading hikers through dense rainforest, steep mountain slopes, muddy paths, rivers, and small villages. It is well known for its importance during World War II, when intense fighting took place along the trail in 1942, giving the trek deep historical meaning.
